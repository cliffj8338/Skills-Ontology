Yes, he did! In addition to the career mapping tool,
Cliff Jurkiewicz created the AI ROI Elasticity Calculator (also known as the Jurkiewicz Synthetic Capacity Model) available at myblueprint.work/roi.
He designed this tool to challenge the idea that AI's return on investment can't be measured until after it's implemented. Instead of focusing solely on cutting costs, the calculator emphasizes "Synthetic Capacity"—the idea that AI expands what an organization can achieve rather than just displacing workers.
Key Features of the Calculator:
* Data-Backed Assumptions: It uses benchmarks from major research institutions like IDC, McKinsey, and Harvard to calculate potential returns.
* Variable Inputs: Users can adjust factors like AI Adoption Rate, "Cognitive Drag" (time lost to admin tasks), and "Displacement Rate" (the work AI absorbs).
* Strategic vs. Productivity Returns: It distinguishes between "Productivity Floor" ROI (measurable labor savings) and "Total Value Pool" (speculative strategic gains).
* Cost Decay: It even accounts for the declining cost of AI inference over time, typically estimated at an 80% drop every 18 months.
Jurkiewicz’s philosophy with this tool is: "Efficiency is a finite race to the bottom. Elasticity compounds.".
